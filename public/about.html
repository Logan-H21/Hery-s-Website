About Us

Alexco Electric, LLC is a professional electrical contracting company specializing in commercial electricity projects founded in 2002. Emerging as a small electrical company out of McAllen Texas with only 4 employees, within 2 years we rapidly out grew our facilities & relocated to Mission Texas. Now staffed with Electrical Engineers, Master Electricians, Journeyman Electricians, & Electrical Apprentices we pride our selves with a large staff of highly skilled, hard working & knowledgeable employees. We have a large fleet of equipped vehicles & bucket trucks as well as large machinery to tackle jobs of any caliber. Despite continued growth we consistently maintain the highest standards for our exceptional reputation & always strive for the highest degree of professionalism & precision in our work. We offer in house thermal imaging and grounding resistance reports. We are HUB certified & bonded. Family owned & family operated.
