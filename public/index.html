Employee Benefits

Medical Insurance

We prioritize the health and well-being of our employees. That's why we offer comprehensive medical insurance to ensure our team has access to quality healthcare. This benefit helps cover medical expenses, offering peace of mind and financial protection for employees and their families, so they can focus on their work and well-being.

Vacation Days

Alexco Electric values a healthy work-life balance, which is why we provide generous vacation days to our employees. We believe that time to rest and recharge is essential for productivity and overall well-being. Our vacation policy ensures that our team can enjoy personal time off to relax, spend time with loved ones, and return to work feeling refreshed.

We recognize the importance of spending time with family and celebrating special occasions. That's why we offer paid holidays to our employees, allowing them to enjoy and observe important national holidays without the worry of missing a paycheck. We believe in providing time off to celebrate and relax throughout the year.

Bonuses

We reward hard work and dedication with performance-based bonuses. Our bonus program is designed to recognize and celebrate the contributions of our employees, offering additional financial incentives for meeting or exceeding company goals. It's our way of saying thank you for your commitment to excellence and helping us grow as a team.
